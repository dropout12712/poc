About the Game

Neon Shift is a speedrun-focused, side-scrolling platformer with a twist! Run, jump and slide your way through both official and player-created levels as fast as possible.

Then, shift into a phantom that can uncover new routes with a dash and chase yourself down to secure a spot on the leaderboard. Master precision platforming and strategic shifting to shave milliseconds off your time!
